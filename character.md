# Eric Snow — Character Sheet

## Identity
- **Name:** Eric Snow (goes by Ser Eric)
- **Epithet:** "The Bold" (self-styled, half-ironic, half-aspiration)
- **Race:** Human
- **Class:** Fighter (Level 1)
- **Background:** Outlander (bastard-born, raised rough)
- **Alignment:** Neutral Good (striving toward it, anyway)

## Origin
Bastard son of a minor Northern lord. His lordship's wife couldn't stomach the sight of him, so he was sent away to squire young. Squired for an itinerant hedge knight — a decent swordsman, a poor companion, now a dead man. Got his knighthood before the fever took the old bastard. Complicated feelings about that. Rides south now. No plan. No lord. No illusions.
