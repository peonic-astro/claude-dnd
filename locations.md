# Known Locations — Eric Snow Campaign

## Current Location

### The Muddy Wheel — Tavern
- **Region:** The Riverlands, a day's ride from the Trident
- **Description:** Low-ceilinged, smoke-stained, smells of tallow and sour ale. Rushes on the floor that haven't been changed since last harvest. A fire that never quite gets the damp out of the air. The kind of place where nobody asks questions because nobody cares enough to.
- **Notable:** Ser Aldric Pyke was waked here two nights past. Three silvers bought a night's drinking for half a dozen people who mostly didn't know him. Eric slept at the table.
- **Owner:** Unknown as yet.
