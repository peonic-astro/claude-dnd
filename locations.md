# Known Locations — Eric Snow Campaign

## Current Location

### Aldyn's Farm / Village (unnamed)
- **Region:** The Riverlands, near the Muddy Wheel tavern
- **Description:** A small cluster of farms, the kind of place that doesn't have a proper name on any map. Low stone walls, muddy yards, smoke from morning fires. Hardworking people with not enough margin for trouble.
- **Notable:** Where Eric based himself for Session 1. Aldyn's barn served as temporary lodging and prisoner holding.

---

## Visited Locations

### The Muddy Wheel — Tavern
- **Region:** The Riverlands, a day's ride from the Trident
- **Description:** Low-ceilinged, smoke-stained, smells of tallow and sour ale. The rushes haven't been changed since last harvest. A fire that never quite gets the damp out of the air. The kind of place where nobody asks questions because nobody cares enough to.
- **Notable:** Ser Aldric Pyke was waked here. Eric slept at the table. Where this story started.

### The Bandit Camp (destroyed)
- **Region:** Woods approximately three miles east of the village
- **Description:** A rough camp — rope corral for horses, two crude shelters, a central fire. Accessed via a dry ditch running along the north field edge.
- **Status:** Burned. Session 1.
- **Notable:** Where the letter was found. Where the woman was captured.
