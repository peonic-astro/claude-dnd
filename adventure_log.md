# Adventure Log — Eric Snow Campaign

## Campaign Overview
Ser Eric Snow, newly knighted hedge knight and bastard of the North, rides south with a dead master behind him and nothing ahead but the road.

---

## Session 1 — The Muddy Wheel & The Bandit Camp

**Location:** The Muddy Wheel tavern and surrounding Riverlands
**Time:** Morning to late evening, late autumn
**Party:** Eric Snow, Berengar (archer, Seagard veteran), Cobb (farmer), Edwyn Hobb (young woodcutter)

### What Happened

Eric woke the morning after Ser Aldric Pyke's wake — poorly attended, money mostly spent, head sore. In the tavern yard while tending Doubt, he overheard two farmers arguing. Aldyn (older, stubborn) and Cobb (younger, worried) were debating what to do about a band of brigands shaking down local farms for silver every fortnight. Eric introduced himself and struck a deal: two silvers to deal with the problem, provided Aldyn furnished three able men.

The party assembled in Aldyn's barn: Berengar (lean, quiet, hunting bow, fought at Seagard in the Greyjoy Rebellion), Cobb (hatchet, honest about his limitations), and Edwyn Hobb (barely twenty, broad as a door, woodcutter's axe, previously felled a horse thief with a fence post). Not a war party. Good enough.

Eric's plan: scout first, reduce the odds quietly, then hit the camp hard and loud. Berengar approved. They approached through a dry ditch along the field edge, left Cobb and Hobb with orders to come running at the sound of fighting.

**The approach and scouting:**
- Berengar spotted a smoke thread and a sentry inside the tree line. Too far and too hooded for a clean bow shot in the rain.
- Eric moved through the ditch and took the sentry from behind. Clean. Quiet. The camp continued unaware.
- Berengar identified a second man with the horses to the north.
- The horse guard turned out to be a boy — seventeen or eighteen, thin, frightened, clearly not there by choice. Eric knocked him out with a reversed pommel, tied him up with his own underdrawers, and left him hidden.
- A third man was found crouching in the trees, otherwise occupied. Eric dealt with him before he could return to camp.

**The assault:**
Three left at the fire, plus an unaccounted sixth — a woman, flagged by Aldyn as the one who did the talking on collection visits. She wasn't visible.

Berengar put an arrow through the largest man (standing, keeping watch). Eric came in hard and loud, screaming for Hobb and Cobb, drove a second man into the fire. The third drew steel and put up a fight — competent, soldier's instincts — but Eric opened him up across the arm and chest and he went down.

Hobb and Cobb arrived crashing out of the tree line, magnificent in their gracelessness. The woman stepped from her shelter and assessed the situation with dark, quick eyes. Held her long knife for one beat too long. Then set it in the mud.

**After the fighting:**
The fire-damaged man was alive but badly burned — arm and shoulder, face scorched. He wouldn't survive the road back. Eric said the old Northern words and sent him on. Nobody commented.

The camp was searched. Cobb found a saddlebag with silver stags and gold dragons — the accumulated take from local farms. Edwyn found a recurve bow, a rolled document, and a sword with a crane-in-flight stamped into the pommel.

The document was a letter, water-damaged but legible. Key contents:
- Delivery of men to Stony Sept by the new moon, or arrangement is void
- *"Lord C—"* wants something delivered
- *"The Snow bastard is not to be harmed. Crane wants him found, not broken."*
- No signature. Broken seal — a bird, possibly a crane.

The woman confirmed it when pressed: she'd been hired to find Eric, not rob farmers. The robbery was freelancing to cover costs while they searched. Lord Crane sent her. She volunteered that the boy with the horses wasn't really one of them.

Camp burned. Coin taken for redistribution. Knight's sword kept. Recurve bow to Berengar. Prisoners — the woman and the boy — brought back to the village under guard. Horses acquired (four animals).

The party returned to the village. Coin argued over in Aldyn's kitchen. Prisoners secured in the barn under Edwyn's watch. Eric slept in the barn, letter against his chest.

**Session ended:** Eric at rest in Aldyn's barn, dawn breaking.

### Key Outcomes
- Bandit problem resolved. Two dead (knife work), one dead (arrow), one dead (mercy kill). Woman captured. Boy captured.
- Eric's payment: 2 silver stags (owed, not yet collected)
- Recovered coin to be returned to farmers
- Knight's sword with Crane crest — in Eric's possession
- Letter — in Eric's possession, against his chest
- Lord Crane is actively looking for Eric Snow. Has been for some time.
- Berengar knows about the letter. Aldyn does not.
