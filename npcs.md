# Known NPCs — Eric Snow Campaign

*This file tracks only what Eric knows. No DM secrets here.*

---

## Allies / Associates

### Berengar
- **Status:** Alive. Farmer/hunter, local to the area.
- **Relationship:** Fought alongside Eric in Session 1. Solid, dependable. Knows about the letter — has said nothing to the others.
- **Appearance:** Lean, forties, hunting bow, watchful eyes. Economical with words.
- **History:** Fought at Seagard during the Greyjoy Rebellion. Not a man who needs to prove himself.
- **Notes:** The most capable of the three men. Asked Eric directly if he knew why Crane wanted him. Trustworthy so far — but trust is early.

### Aldyn
- **Status:** Alive. Farmer, village elder of sorts.
- **Relationship:** Hired Eric. Paid 2 silver stags (owed).
- **Appearance:** Older, thick-shouldered, white stubble, weathered. Direct to the point of bluntness.
- **Notes:** Does NOT know about the letter. Berengar advised keeping it that way. Has some history with the woman — something in the way he looked at her.

### Cobb
- **Status:** Alive. Local farmer.
- **Relationship:** Part of the party for Session 1. Honest about his limitations.
- **Appearance:** Younger than Aldyn, maybe thirty. Carries a hatchet.
- **Notes:** Reliable enough. Not a fighter but didn't run.

### Edwyn Hobb
- **Status:** Alive. Local, barely twenty.
- **Relationship:** Part of the party for Session 1. Fell asleep on watch (sitting up, axe on knees — close enough).
- **Appearance:** Broad as a doorway. Woodcutter's axe. Strong, earnest.
- **Notes:** Knocked a horse thief cold with a fence post at eighteen. More capable than he looks, which is saying something.

## Prisoners

### The Woman (name unknown)
- **Status:** Alive. Bound. In Aldyn's barn.
- **Relationship:** Leader of the bandit band. Sent by Lord Crane to find Eric.
- **Appearance:** Mid-thirties. Sharp-featured, dark hair, lean. Road-weathered. Holds herself straight even with her hands tied.
- **Known:** Was hired to find "the Snow bastard" — alive and unharmed, per Crane's instructions. The robbery was freelancing to cover costs. Has a tactical mind. Volunteered that the boy wasn't really one of hers.
- **Notes:** Knows more than she's said. Interrogation pending Session 2.

### Pate (the boy with the horses)
- **Status:** Alive. Bound. In Aldyn's barn. Asleep.
- **Relationship:** Coerced member of the band. Not there by choice.
- **Appearance:** Seventeen or eighteen. Thin in the shoulders. Too-big sword.
- **Known:** The woman confirmed he wasn't really one of them. Coerced somehow.
- **Notes:** Probably from one of the local farming families. To be determined.

## Deceased

### Ser Aldric Pyke (former master)
- **Status:** Dead. Fever. One week ago.
- **Relationship:** Eric's knight-master. Did not like him much. Taught him well enough. Gave him his knighthood on his deathbed.
- **Appearance:** Wiry, weathered, hawkish face, always squinting.
- **Notes:** Saddlebags not yet fully searched. May be carrying something of significance.

## Known Of (Not Met)

### Lord Crane (Lord Edwyn Crane)
- **Status:** Alive. Minor Riverlands lord.
- **Known:** Has a small keep approximately two days east. Actively looking for Eric Snow — sent the woman's band to find him. Letter says Eric is not to be harmed. Reason unknown.
- **Berengar says:** Minor lord, more pride than land. Fought in the Rebellion without distinction. Struggling financially. Has been unable to keep a household knight.
- **Notes:** His crest is a crane in flight. The recovered sword bears this mark.
