# Known NPCs — Eric Snow Campaign

*This file tracks only what Eric knows. No DM secrets here.*

---

## Allies / Associates

### Berengar
- **Status:** Alive. Travelling with Eric.
- **Relationship:** Fought alongside Eric in Session 1. Riding to Stony Sept. Knows about the Crane letter — has said nothing to the others.
- **Appearance:** Lean, forties, hunting bow, watchful eyes. Economical with words.
- **History:** Fought at Seagard during the Greyjoy Rebellion. Not a man who needs to prove himself.
- **Notes:** Was already packed when Eric asked him to ride east. That says something.

### Aldyn
- **Status:** Alive. Farmer, village elder of sorts.
- **Relationship:** Hired Eric. Paid 2 silver stags.
- **Appearance:** Older, thick-shouldered, white stubble, weathered. Direct to the point of bluntness.
- **Notes:** Mira is his cousin's daughter. He knew it when they brought her in and said nothing — called it cowardice when Eric pressed him. Has taken responsibility for her now. Paid his debts without fumbling.

### Cobb
- **Status:** Alive. Local farmer.
- **Relationship:** Part of the party for Session 1. Honest about his limitations.
- **Appearance:** Younger than Aldyn, maybe thirty. Carries a hatchet.
- **Notes:** Reliable enough. Not a fighter but didn't run.

### Edwyn Hobb
- **Status:** Alive. Local, barely twenty. Now has a horse.
- **Relationship:** Part of the party for Session 1. Given the dun mare by Eric.
- **Appearance:** Broad as a doorway. Woodcutter's axe. Strong, earnest.
- **Notes:** Fell asleep on watch. Red-eared with gratitude about the horse. Said it to his boots.

### Marta Wrenby
- **Status:** Alive. Farmer, east of the village.
- **Relationship:** Aunt of Pate. Came for him without fuss.
- **Appearance:** Broad, capable. Flour on her hands when she arrived.
- **Notes:** Didn't ask Eric for much. Took Pate home. Real loyalty if Eric ever needs it — he returned something she'd been quietly fearing was lost.

### Tam Wren
- **Status:** Alive. Farmer.
- **Relationship:** Bought the bay cob from Eric. 4 silver now, 2 in spring.
- **Notes:** A man who pays his debts. Daughter named the horse Biscuit.

## Released / Resolved

### Mira
- **Status:** Alive. In Aldyn's custody.
- **Relationship:** Leader of Harwick's band, sent by Crane to find Eric. Captured Session 1, released to Aldyn Session 2.
- **Appearance:** Mid-thirties. Sharp-featured, dark hair, lean. Road-weathered. Holds herself straight even with her hands tied.
- **Known:** Was hired through Crane's steward Ryck in Saltpans. She read the brief personally — grey roan, one white sock, hedge knight called Snow, bring him to Stony Sept. Doesn't know why Crane wants Eric. The robbery was freelancing to cover costs while they searched. Kept Harwick from burning things.
- **Notes:** Told Eric everything she knew in exchange for a word with Aldyn. Aldyn is her father's cousin — she's blood, she's trouble, she's now working off what she owes.

### Pate Wrenby
- **Status:** Alive. With Marta Wrenby.
- **Relationship:** Coerced member of the band. Not there by choice.
- **Appearance:** Nineteen. Thin, dark circles. Frightened more than criminal.
- **Known:** Owed money in Fairmarket. Harwick collected the debt in labour. Tended the horses, kept out of the collecting. Knows the route Harwick's band worked — will help the village recover what was taken.
- **Notes:** His aunt knows now. He'll have some accounting to do.

## Deceased

### Ser Aldric Pyke (former master)
- **Status:** Dead. Fever. Approximately ten days ago.
- **Relationship:** Eric's knight-master. Did not like him much. Taught him well enough. Gave him his knighthood on his deathbed.
- **Appearance:** Wiry, weathered, hawkish face, always squinting.
- **Notes:** Saddlebags searched Session 2. Hidden letter found in the lining — signed A.P. Concerned a child, a woman named Essara, and something hidden. Aldric trusted Eric to know what to do. Eric doesn't, yet.

## Known Of (Not Met)

### Lord Crane (Lord Edwyn Crane)
- **Status:** Alive. Minor Riverlands lord.
- **Known:** Has a small keep. Actively looking for Eric Snow — sent Mira's band to find him. Letter says Eric is not to be harmed. Meeting point is Stony Sept, not Crane's keep. His steward Ryck arranged things in Saltpans. Reason for seeking Eric unknown.
- **Notes:** Crest is a crane in flight. The recovered sword bears this mark.

### Ryck (Crane's steward)
- **Status:** Alive, presumably.
- **Known:** Made the arrangement with Mira in Saltpans. Made a point of saying the coin was good — twice. Particular about Stony Sept as the meeting point.
- **Notes:** Practical man, sounds like.

### Essara of the Blue Lamp, Leyrmont
- **Status:** Unknown.
- **Known:** Named in Aldric's hidden letter. A woman in Leyrmont, Essos, who was given a child to care for. She doesn't know who the father was — only the mother's name.
- **Notes:** Eric found this name in Aldric's letter. Has no other context.
